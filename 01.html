VHS

Introduced in 1976, the VHS (Video Home System) was the dominant video format in the 80s and 90s. In the midst of the format wars between Betamax and other analog formats beforehand, the VHS ended up being the victor for its consumer friendliness and with it being more affordable than its competitors. While it is considered obsolete technology wise, people are still fond of VHS due to nostalgia but also the picture quality. It may not be the highest quality picture but the boxed resolution and the fuzzy picture gives an aesthetic that many still use to this day to recapture their “glory days”.
