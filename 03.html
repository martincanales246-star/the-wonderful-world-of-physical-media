Blu-Ray

Serving as the successor to DVD, the Blu-Ray was introduced in 2006 to provide a higher quality and more convenient experience. The biggest difference between Blu-Ray and DVD is the picture quality and storage capability. DVDs only displayed in 480i while Blu-Rays displayed in 1080p which at the time, was the highest quality picture. Not only that, Blu-Ray discs have over 15GB of space with DVDs only having 4GB, meaning there is more space for more bonus content for publishers to add. Blu-Rays are picture perfect!
