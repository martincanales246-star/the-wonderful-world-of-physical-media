DVD

DVDs (Digital Video Disc) were a giant leap in the home video industry and the format that is still used to this very day. Introduced in 1996, these discs were able to hold 4GB worth of video. That means that not only were publishers able to fit not just the content published, but also a ton of other bonuses that people still fondly remember to this day. Some of these bonuses include extra videos like behind the scenes documentaries for movies or animated menus with several options for the user to choose from. It was like seeing a sneak peek into the universe of the movie you're about to watch in just a single disc!
